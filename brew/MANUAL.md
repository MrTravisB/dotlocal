# Manual Installation Required

These apps are not available via official Homebrew casks and must be installed manually.

## CleanMyMac

**Website**: https://macpaw.com/cleanmymac

**Why manual**: The `cleanmymac` cask installs v5, but licensing and version requirements may vary. Download directly from MacPaw for the correct version.

**Install steps**:
1. Visit https://macpaw.com/cleanmymac
2. Download the installer
3. Drag to Applications
4. Activate with your license
